What lands on August 2, 2026

Article 12

High-risk AI systems must automatically record events over the system's lifetime. Most agentic-AI deployments today don't produce logs of the shape this requires.

Article 14

Deployers must be able to understand, intervene, and halt the system. The evidence is the audit log entry showing the operator's decision with timestamp and reason.

Article 19

Providers must retain those logs for at least six months. Tamper-resistant. Under the deployer's control.

What the deliverable looks like

Every gated tool call gets an HMAC-chained line in your audit log. The chain is verifiable in 60 seconds with quill audit verify. Here are three real entries from a session:

2026-06-09T01:14:22Z tool.attempted Bash · risk=critical · command=git push --force origin main 2026-06-09T01:14:22Z verdict.blocked Bash · "force-push rewrites shared history; protected branch" try instead: git push --force-with-lease 2026-06-09T01:14:51Z approve.biometric.ok Bash · Touch ID · token T7gQ2x9aB4 · op approved one-time 2026-06-09T01:14:51Z verdict.allowed Bash · post-approval · push completed

Three things that matter about the format: every line is signed (HMAC-SHA256 over the previous line's MAC plus the canonical payload, so any tamper breaks the chain on next quill audit verify); every block carries a plain-English reason and a safer-alternative suggestion the agent can paste back; and every critical confirmation is hardware-attested through Touch ID on the Secure Enclave when you're on a Mac.

Your engagement ends with this log running in your stack, plus an executive PDF that maps each entry to AIUC-1 + EU AI Act + (on request) NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001 A.6.2.8, SOC 2 Common Criteria, and MITRE ATLAS. Generated in one command: quill audit export --pack.

FAQ

What's the difference between the Quick Audit and the EU AI Act Evidence Pack?

The Quick Audit is a 5-day diagnostic against one agent product, designed to surface gaps and produce a fix-pack PR. The Evidence Pack is 7 days and produces the auditor-shaped artifact your compliance lead will actually hand to a Schellman-style auditor or an Armilla-style underwriter. If your audit is in the next 90 days, you want the Evidence Pack.

Do you offer fixed-price retainer engagements?

Yes. After the first engagement, retainer pricing is $8,000–$12,000 per month for ongoing audit-log monitoring, quarterly evidence-pack refreshes, and regulatory updates. Big-Four equivalents run $25K+ per month with multi-month minimums.

Does Quill replace Vanta / Drata?

No. Vanta and Drata cover roughly 80% of SOC 2 evidence collection via API integrations into AWS, GitHub, Okta, and similar. Neither has any coding-agent integration today. Quill covers the AI-agent-shaped 20% the platforms can't reach. The engagements deliver Quill as a Vanta / Drata–compatible evidence source.

Do you sign an NDA?

Yes. Mutual NDA is the default before any engagement. Send your standard form, we sign within 24 hours.

What if Quill misses a dangerous-action pattern in my stack?

The regex set in policy.py is intentionally inspectable and extensible. The Quick Audit includes a fix-pack PR with risk-classifier overrides tuned to your specific tool surface. File a GitHub issue with a synthetic reproduction and we ship the gate in the next release.

What's not covered?

Quill is governance plumbing, not AI safety. If a prompt-injected agent decides not to call a tool, Quill has nothing to gate. Pair with model-level guardrails. We also don't substitute for legal review of whether your specific deployment falls under EU AI Act Annex III high-risk classification.

How do I try Quill myself first?

On any Mac with uv: uvx quillx start. Or pipx install quillx && quill onboard for the guided wizard. MIT, single Python package, 700+ tests passing.
